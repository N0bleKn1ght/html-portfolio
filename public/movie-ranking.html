Best Animes of All Time According to Evelio

My top 3 Animes

1. Full Metal Alchmist

Fullmetal Alchemist" follows the journey of two brothers, Edward and Alphonse Elric, who use alchemy in their quest to restore their bodies after a failed alchemical experiment to bring their mother back to life. The brothers face various challenges, including encounters with the military, homunculi, and the moral implications of their actions, all while seeking the Philosopher's Stone, a powerful object that could help them achieve their goal.

2. Attack on Titan

Attack on Titan" is set in a world where humanity resides within enormous walled cities to protect themselves from Titans, gigantic humanoid creatures that devour humans. The story follows Eren Yeager, his adoptive sister Mikasa Ackerman, and their friend Armin Arlert as they join the military to fight against the Titans after their hometown is invaded, leading to a quest for revenge and the truth behind the Titans' existence.

3. Death Note

"Death Note" follows the story of Light Yagami, a highly intelligent high school student who discovers a mysterious notebook that allows him to kill anyone by writing their name in it while picturing their face. Using the Death Note, Light aims to rid the world of criminals and create a utopia where he reigns as a god. However, his actions attract the attention of law enforcement and a brilliant detective known only as "L," leading to a high-stakes game of cat and mouse.
